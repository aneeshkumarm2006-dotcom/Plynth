01/How it works Three steps from deal to funding
01

Submit the deal

Enter the property and loan details, or drop the documents — Plynth reads the appraisal and MLS and proposes the terms for you to confirm. Borrowers stay anonymized by default.

02

Receive matched offers

Your deal is matched against the standing criteria of every relevant lender. Competing offers arrive with rate, fees, term and conditions laid out side by side.

03

Accept, counter, fund

Negotiate within Plynth, accept the offer that fits, and track the file to funding. Every closed deal builds a verifiable track record you carry forward.
